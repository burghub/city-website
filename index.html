Top three activities to do in Amsterdam

Explore the Canals

Take a boat tour or rent a kayak to experience the city's iconic canals and picturesque bridges.

Visit the Museums

Discover world-class art at the Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum, and Anne Frank House.

Stroll through Jordaan

Wander through this charming neighborhood filled with narrow streets, boutique shops, and cozy cafes.
